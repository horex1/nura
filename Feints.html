Feints

And also the flight of the ball depends on where you kick the ball.

They say that techies are not made, but born. But it is not so. No even the most
technical player is born that way. Even if you have talent, you still need to work
on its development and polish your skills in training and in games. How to learn
to feint in football? You may not be the next Messi, but you will learn something.

Marseille roulette

In the 80s, this feint was best performed by Maradona, and in the next generation
Zinedine Zidane perfectly mastered it. That is why many people call this movement
"Zidane's feint". The player, as it were, becomes one foot on the ball, while makinga
rotational movement 360 degrees and putting the other foot on the ball.

In front of the opponent, the back is first exposed, and then it becomes possible to
go into open space. Agility and speed are decisive here, and the slightest mistake
threatens to lose the ball.
