Ball control

In football, it is very important to be able to control the ball and control it. Dribbling,
or dribbling, is one of the fundamental aspects of football, without which it is difficult
to give a good pass or shoot on goal. Do you want to better master the art of dribbling?
There are many different techniques and techniques that involve different areas of
both feet. Having mastered them, you will learn how to better control the ball, maneuver
faster and move around the football field.

Dribbling is carried out with the pads of the fingers, the fingers should be relaxed and
spread out, a semicircular movement is made with the hand, while the forearm moves
up and down. Dribbling masters dribble close to the ground, which reduces the risk
of being intercepted by a defender.
